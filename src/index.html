Roadmap

This roadmap outlines our goals. We want to take our NFT's to where no one else has before. Our ideas and concepts are inspired from 3D, Augmented Reality & AI. This roadmap can evolve over time according to our community needs!

.01

Launch

The digitally generated world of Artemis is hand crafted with a team of 3D Artists, Sound Designers and Computational Magicians.

The uniquely crafted "8879" spirit animals have their special powers. We are working closely with our community online who are all ready to become Artemesians & to finalise The Drop release with them.

.02

Real World Integration

Our NFT's go beyond screens and intergrate into the real world of its owners. We are creating specially designed T-shirts powered by Augmented Reality.

Your spirit animals come alive with their super powers available to the owners when they wear the T-Shirt. We aim to create more clothing & merchandise powered by Augmented Reality, helping you interact with the Artemis metaverse

.03

Artemis & Artists

We are part of Art community which goes beyond digital art and believe in reviving forgotten traditions and perspectives.

We will develop a series of Artemis art works in collaborations with Tribal artists of Ghana, New Zealand, Australia, India etc., and introduce them to the NFT world. We envision to share income generated from the art works and help Tribal communities across the world.

.04

Artemis Metaverse

After our first drop of spirit animals, we will extend the Artemis digital world by sharing unexplored dimensions of the planet, wilder jungles, rarer animals and their powers.

Ownership in the Artemis Metaverse will extend buying lands, breeding animals, growing food and owning spirit powers, which makes the Artemis Metaverse more immersive and fun.

.05

The Future

In the near future, our efforts will be focused on maintaining the Metaverse, structuring citizenship process of the planet, introducing more interesting animals and lands to own and nurture. We will also be working on developing more immersive AR experience for Earthlings.
